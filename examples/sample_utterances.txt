# ── Grief and loss ──────────────────────────────────────────
I never told my mother I was angry at her. She died before I could say it.
She used to sing that to me when I was scared. I haven't heard it since she died.
He never said he was proud of me. Not once. And I keep doing things anyway.

# ── Vulnerability ────────────────────────────────────────────
My hands shake when I think about going back home.
I am tired. Not sleepy tired. Something deeper.
Sometimes I pretend to be fine just to give everyone a break from me.

# ── Unresolved feelings ──────────────────────────────────────
I don't understand why it still hurts. It was so long ago.
When he left, I thought — finally. And then I cried for three days.
I thought if I worked hard enough I could fix it. I couldn't.

# ── Longing ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
There's this moment in every song where I feel like everything might be okay.
I miss the version of myself that believed things would get better.
I keep the letters but I can't read them anymore.

# ── Conflict ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
I love her but I don't like who I am around her.
Part of me wants to forgive him. Part of me thinks that would be a betrayal.
I stayed too long. I knew it and I stayed anyway.

# ── Warmth / connection ──────────────────────────────────────
When my daughter holds my hand I feel like I haven't ruined everything.
There are three people in this world who I would do anything for.
Sometimes a meal is enough. Just being in the same kitchen is enough.

# ── Tension / fierce ─────────────────────────────────────────
I am done carrying things that were never mine.
They called it strength but it was just survival.
I refuse to let what happened to me happen to her.

# ── Absence / numbness ───────────────────────────────────────
I stopped feeling things at some point and I'm not sure when.
I go through the days. That's all I can say right now.
Nothing feels real. Not bad, just — not there.
